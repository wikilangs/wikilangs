## [0.1.1] - 2025-12-27

Initial release of wikilangs: pre-trained tokenizers, n-gram models, Markov chains, vocabularies, and embeddings for 340+ languages. Built for researchers, educators, and developers..

### Added
- `wikilangs` package: tokenization, vocabulary, n-gram and Markov model utilities
- Prebuilt tokenizer and subword artifacts
- Embeddings utilities and simple monolingual embedding support
- Examples and demo notebooks under `examples/`
- Tests covering tokenizer, ngram, markov, vocabulary and embeddings
- A small website and scripts for fetching language pages in `website/`

- See README.md for usage instructions, data download guidance, and contribution notes.
